# The Molecular Code of Conduct
## Core Philosophy

As a community, we pledge to develop towards an international environment where our bodies, as people negated by systems of exploitation, can be expressed, and consequently can be driven to produce against means of repression. We pledge to fight against the systems which funnel gender, sexuality, race, caste, and class into assimilation and negation. Bigotry will not be tolerated within our community, under our collective fight for expression over repression.

This means we deny the notion that accessibility should be assimilated into a closed ecosystem, where only the privileged can obtain the means to make their lives accessible, and where the public cannot orchestrate the means which produce accessibility themselves. We want a democracy of the production behind accessibility, and we will fight for it as a source of expression in a world that negates and represses our bodies, and the relationship our bodies have with the world.

We do not tolerate the colonial settler projects of the west, in their eugenicist project to assimilate our bodies and our desire into their accepted structures. 

This means we will not tolerate the Israeli, Zionist settler project as it decimates the people of Palestine. This means we will not tolerate the material exploitation of the “third-world” as they are forced into western servitude. This means we will not tolerate the negation of the queer subject. This means we will fight against the forces that manufacture race; the forces that set forth racism through negation. This means we will fight for our bodies, as a collective. And in this fight, accessibility will be our vector for change.

We will hold ourselves accountable to abide by these fundamental principles of thought.

By joining the Molecular Discord server, opening an issue, or contributing to our project, you sign off on this pledge, and are expected to hold yourself accountable under our code of conduct.
